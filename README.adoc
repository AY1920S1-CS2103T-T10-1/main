== What is JARVIS
JARVIS is a productivity manager tool for NUS students.

As a NUS student, are'nt you overwhelmed with all the different aspects of your life?
Managing your schedule, finances and mapping your modules can be very time consuming and confusing.
JARVIS is here to help you macro manage all these aspects in your daily life.
JARVIS will help you track your calendar, your to-dos, manage your budget and plan out your semesters, all in one place!
